Defaqto Five Star Rated Insurance

We work hard to make car insurance easy. Easy to manage online. Easy to make a claim. Plus, with our highly-rated cover, you can get on the road with peace of mind. Welcome to easy car insurance.

Courtesy car

If you have an accident, our approved repairer will pick up your car, fix it, valet it and return it to you. They'll even deliver a courtesy car for you to use while yours is being repaired.

Uninsured Driver Promise

If you're in an accident with an uninsured driver, it shouldn't cost you a penny when you claim. That's why we'll reimburse your excess and your NCD will be reinstated.

Onward travel cover to UK destination

If your car is damaged in an accident and you're unable to drive it, we can arrange transport for you and your passengers to get home or to your planned UK destination.
